FAQ

Questions Before
We Start?

Here are the main things most brands ask before starting a project.

How much does a project cost?

+

Projects usually range from $100 to $10,000, depending on what you need. A few product images cost less. A full product video, ad, or large campaign costs more. Once I understand your product, style, and deadline, I’ll send a clear quote before we start.

How long does delivery take?

+

Delivery can take anywhere from 24 hours to 20 days, depending on the size of the project. Simple product images can be faster. Bigger videos need more time for planning, motion, polish, and final export. I’ll always share a clear timeline before the project begins.

How many revisions are included?

+

Each project includes 1 revision. This helps us fine-tune the final work after the first delivery. Extra revisions are charged hourly because 3D changes often take time to adjust, preview, render, and polish.

What counts as a revision?

+

A revision means small changes to improve the work, like adjusting colors, text, timing, product position, or small visual details. A full change in idea, scene, product style, or video direction may need a new quote.

Why are extra revisions charged hourly?

+

3D work is not like editing a simple image. Even small changes can need setup, testing, rendering, and final polish. Charging hourly for extra revisions keeps the process fair and helps protect the project timeline.

Do you work with long-term clients?

+

Yes. I’m open to one-time projects and long-term work for brands that need regular product images, videos, and ad creatives.

Can you make videos for Instagram and ads?

+

Yes. I can create short product videos for Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, website banners, and paid ads.
